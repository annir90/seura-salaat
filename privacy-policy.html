Introduction

Seura Salaat ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile prayer times application handles your information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Seura Salaat is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Device information
  • Contact lists
  • Photos or media files

How the App Works

Seura Salaat operates primarily offline and does not require an internet connection for core functionality:

  • Prayer times are calculated locally on your device
  • All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties
  • No user accounts or registration required

Local Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications to remind you of prayer times. These notifications:

  • Are generated entirely on your device
  • Do not involve any external servers
  • Can be disabled at any time through your device settings
  • Do not collect or transmit any data

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Prayer time settings
  • Notification preferences
  • App customization options

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

Third-Party Services

Seura Salaat does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app functions independently without external dependencies that could compromise your privacy.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age, as we do not collect any personal information from any users.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through data breaches on our end.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and will take effect immediately upon posting.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data for you to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All your app data remains under your control on your device.
